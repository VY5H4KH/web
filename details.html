Interstellar

2014

Rating 8.7/10

Director: Christopher Nolan
Writers: Jonathan Nolan, Christopher Nolan
Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

"Interstellar" is a 2014 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts, led by Cooper (played by Matthew McConaughey), who embark on a space voyage through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity, as Earth is facing environmental collapse. The film explores complex themes of time dilation, relativity, and human survival in the face of cosmic challenges. It features stunning visual effects, a haunting soundtrack by Hans Zimmer, and an ensemble cast including Anne Hathaway and Jessica Chastain. "Interstellar" received critical acclaim for its thought-provoking narrative and scientific accuracy.
